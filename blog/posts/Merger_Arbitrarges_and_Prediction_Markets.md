


### Mergers

How do David Ellison, Donald Trump, Kalshi, and the rights to *the Sopranos* all come together? Larry Ellison. A story which ties these all together is Larry Ellison's, CEO and co-founder of Oracle, modern push for amassing a huge portfolio of the US media landscape — forming a firm foundation of creating new Murdochs. I don't know if it was David Ellison, Larry Ellison's son, which convinced Larry that he should spend tens of billions of dollars amassing the US media industry or if it was the other way around. David Ellison started his life like a lot "affluent" (Larry was a billionare at the time of his birth and currently has a net worth of $ \$245.3$ billion) California people: being into the film industry and going to USC (also my undergraduate institution!). Later on he dropped out of USC and made a box-office bomb, *Flyboys*, financed by Larry, and went on to do a few other small-time acting roles before switching to the buisness side of Hollywood. This move into business came in the form of founding *Skydance Media*, which I have to say is a perfect, corporate name — just think about it.  

Skydance media operated as media production and financing company which covered,  e.g., Mission: Impossible, Top Gun.
